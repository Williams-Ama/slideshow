Who is an Entrepreneur?

An entrepreneur is an individual who identifies opportunities, takes risks, and innovates to create and manage a business venture. They drive economic development and create value in society through their business initiatives.

Impact of Entrepreneurship on Society:

  • Generates employment and economic opportunities.
  • Introduces innovative products and services to the market.
  • Boosts local and global economic growth.
  • Encourages financial independence and wealth creation.
  • Develops problem-solving skills and fosters creativity.
  • Enhances leadership, resilience, and adaptability.
